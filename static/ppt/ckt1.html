We don't care about the gates. We only care about getting the same results. So we the following process

For any operator U U applied to channel ρ \rho let S ( U ) ρ = U ρ U † , S(U)\rho = U\rho U^\dagger, then

S ( U ) = ∑ j 1 . . . j n ∑ k 1 . . . k n S ( U ) j , k ∣ e j 1 . . . e j n ⟩ ⟩ ⟨ ⟨ e k 1 . . . e k n ∣ S(U) = \sum_{j_1...j_n} \sum_{k_1...k_n} S(U)_{j,k} |e_{j_1}...e_{j_n}\rangle\rangle\langle\langle e_{k_1}...e_{k_n}|

Then measurement is: Trace of the action of the Observable on the density matrix i.e T r ( O S ( U ) ρ ) = T r ( O U ρ U † ) Tr(O S(U)\rho) = Tr(O U\rho U^\dagger)
